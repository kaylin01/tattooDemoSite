Aftercare

Keep Your Piercing
Happy & Healthy

Good aftercare is the difference between a quick heal and a troubled one. Here's what we recommend.

01

Saline Rinse Twice Daily

Use a sterile saline solution (0.9% sodium chloride) — spray directly onto the piercing and let it air dry. No cotton buds.

02

Don't Touch It

Avoid touching, rotating, or playing with your jewellery. Bacteria from hands is the leading cause of piercing infections.

03

Avoid Submerging

Stay out of swimming pools, ocean water, and baths for the initial healing period. Showers are perfectly fine.

04

Sleep on Clean Pillowcases

For ear piercings, change your pillowcase every 2–3 days or use a travel pillow to avoid pressure on the piercing.

05

Downsize on Schedule

Initial jewellery is slightly longer to allow for swelling. Come back at 6–8 weeks for a safe downsize — don't do it yourself.

06

Be Patient

Ear piercings can take 6–12 months to fully heal. Body piercings can take up to 18 months. Healing is a journey — we're here for it.
