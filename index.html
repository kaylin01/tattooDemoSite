Post-Ink Care

Tattoo Aftercare

A great tattoo is only as good as how well it heals. Follow these guidelines to protect your investment and keep your ink looking vibrant for years to come.

  • Keep the tattoo covered with cling wrap for the first 2–4 hours after your session.
  • Gently wash with unscented antibacterial soap and warm water twice daily.
  • Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturiser (we recommend Bepanthen) 3× per day.
  • Avoid submerging in pools, the ocean, or baths for at least 3 weeks.
  • Stay out of direct sunlight and apply SPF 50+ once fully healed.
  • Do NOT pick or scratch the peeling skin — let it heal naturally.
  • Wear loose, breathable clothing over the tattooed area while it heals.
