How It Works

Your Tattoo Journey,
Step by Step

We've refined our process to make sure every client leaves feeling confident, cared for, and completely in love with their new ink.

01

Initial Consultation

Book a free 30-minute consultation — in person or via WhatsApp. Share your ideas, reference images, placement preferences, and budget. We listen first, then guide you.

02

Design & Quote

Your chosen artist sketches a custom design based on your brief. You'll receive a detailed quote and a first look at the artwork before you commit to anything.

03

Booking & Deposit

Once you love the design, we lock in your appointment with a small deposit. This secures your slot and goes toward the final cost of your tattoo.

04

The Session

On the day, arrive well-rested and well-fed. We apply the stencil, confirm placement, and get started. Our studio is relaxed and music-friendly — bring headphones or a playlist!

05

Wrap Up & Aftercare

When we're done, we wrap your fresh tattoo and walk you through a full aftercare routine. We'll also send you a digital aftercare guide to keep as a reference.

06

Free Touch-Up

Once your tattoo is fully healed (6–8 weeks), come back for a complimentary touch-up if needed. We stand behind every piece we create — no extra charge.
